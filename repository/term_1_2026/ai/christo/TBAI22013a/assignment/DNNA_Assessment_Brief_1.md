[Deep Neural Network Architectures] (TBAI22013a) 

ASSIGNMENT NO. 1: Deep Learning Implementation Project 

OBJECTIVES: This module introduces deep neural network architectures with a focus on end-to-end learning models including Autoencoders, Deep Belief Networks, GANs, CNNs, Recurrent and Recursive Neural Networks, feature extraction, initialization, fine-tuning, and training techniques. Students will gain practical experience building and training deep learning models.
